Lasagna

Recipe

Lasagna is a classic, comforting baked pasta dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, a rich and savory meat-based tomato sauce, and a creamy cheese filling. The combination of slow-simmered sauce, soft pasta, and melted cheeses creates a hearty, satisfying meal that’s both filling and deeply flavorful

It’s known for its warm, homestyle feel and is often served as a main dish for family dinners or gatherings. With its balanced mix of protein, sauce, and cheese, lasagna is a timeless recipe that feels indulgent yet familiar, and it’s just as good reheated the next day.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • LTomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian herbs
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until soft.
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes and let it simmer for about 15–20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta with an egg, a pinch of salt and pepper, and some grated cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, then ricotta mixture, then meat sauce, and mozzarella.
  8. Repeat the layers until everything is used, finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
